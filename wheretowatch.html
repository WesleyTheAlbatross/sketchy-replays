Where to Watch (Live)

RTBF Auvio

https://auvio.rtbf.be

Commentary is in French, but provides pretty much everything available to watch live.

Tip

One of the best workarounds is to open BBC Radio 5 for English commentary, then mute the French RTBF audio and keep BBC Radio playing in the background.

Other Options

You can also watch replays on other sites like fullraces.com (may have popups).
